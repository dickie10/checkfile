Big Data: Why size matters. And why it matters.
Supported by: EarthServer European Commission Idea and Concept by:

In recent years, "Big Data" has become a new ubiquitous term to express that measured, observed, and generated data are flooding us to an extent that we cannot cope with it. Dealing with Big Data is transforming science, engineering, medicine, healthcare, finance, business, and ultimately society itself.

This film project is about Big Data - bringing them to interested citizens, informing them, and exciting them about the challenges and opportunities science and technology encounter. While focusing on Earth Sciences here - so we are observing our planet, its oceans, air, and lands - the concept is well suited for a mini series where other Big Data areas can be covered as well.
