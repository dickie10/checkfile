CoperniCUBE

European Copernicus Datacube Service

CoperniCUBE will establish massive spatio-temporal Earth Observation datacubes as a commodity for governments and the geospatial industry, based on the leading scalable datacube technology, rasdaman. Flexible mix-and-match based on open Big Geo Datacube standards such as INSPIRE WCS will be brought from scientific trials into everyday use for non-experts and experts alike.

"one cube says more than a million images"
